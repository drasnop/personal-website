Design

This quote from Alan Kay perfectly sums up my philosophy in interaction design:

"Simple things should be simple.
Complex things should be possible."

I recognize the value of minimalism in contemporary design. But I believe we must resist the temptation of uniformity. Typography, color and animations offer endless opportunities to create original experiences.
