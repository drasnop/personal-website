Research

I am a Masters student in Computer Science at UBC, in the Multimodal User Experience lab. My research interests are in the field of Human-Computer Interaction, with a focus on adaptable and adaptive interfaces.

Under the supervision of Professor Joanna McGrenere, I am re-thinking how people customize software, to design a new interaction model—truly user-centric, instead of the current "dirty GUI for a config file".

Design

I love to design and build sleek interfaces, with beautiful colors and subtle animations. This quote from Alan Kay perfectly sums up my philosophy in interaction design:

"Simple things should be simple. Complex things should be possible."
